PROJECTS

SoleMate

SoleMate is a fitness based social media app that leverages geolocation technology to match users with runners in their area. Users are able to message and request runs with people they've matched with, generate customized routes, and track running info in real-time. When users aren't on the app, SoleMate alerts them to incoming requests and messages via push notifications.

Retire Rich

An Electron app that projects how long cash and 401k savings will last after retirement. Users input information about themselves, such as income, spending habits, desired retirement age, etc., in order to generate a graph of their savings over time. Retire Rich also allows users to download a PDF of their projected savings.

TECH STACK

An e-commerce site for selling yachts. Yachts Don't Stop utlizes the technologies listed below to allow users or guests to create carts, continue shiopping, edit their carts, and checkout.
